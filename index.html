TARA HALL

Beautiful Tranquillity

Tara hall is a newly renovated space with plenty of natural light, outside courtyard and fully equipped kitchen.

With 130 square meters of space and a purpose-built stage it is perfect for any small to mid-size function.

Tara Hall is located at the rear of a heritage listed chapel built in 1866 which is now utilized as a Buddhist centre.

Tara hall has a light airy and tranquil ambience and is located only 30 minutes from the city on Magill road.

Seating capacity is 100 and standing 150. AV equipment and seating for up to 100 is available upon request.

Tara Hall is an alcohol and smoke free venue.

FAQs

Q1: Can I inspect the venue? Venue can be inspected between 9.30 -2.30pm Mon to Friday.
Go to Buddha House at the front of the Hall.
There an office volunteer will be happy to show you around.

Q2: How big is Tara Hall or how many people can the hall hold? The hall is 12m long by 8m wide with a small stage.
There is seating room for 80 and it has been measured to hold 35 yoga mats.
More people can be accommodated if seated on the floor space in conjunction with chairs

Q3: Can we access the kitchen?Kitchen hire of $50 gives full access to the kitchen.
If kitchen is not hired, you will have access to a mini fridge and an urn, cutlery, cups and teaspoons.
Hirers provide tea break supplies and clean up after event.

Q4: Are there yoga mats, cushions, and blankets?The basic hire provides trestle tables, chairs, white board and a flip chart.

Q5: Is there Audio/Video equipment, wi-fi?There is free wi-fi.
Audio/Video hire of $50 will provide access to a projector and screen,
PA sound system with a hands-free microphone.

Q6: Is the hall airconditioned?The hall has gas heating and evaporative cooling with fans.
The kitchen has reverse cycle air conditioning.

Q7: Can we use the courtyard garden?Yes, included in hire is full access to the eastern courtyard with outdoor seating

Q8: We are having a fundraiser can people bring their own wine? In line with the Buddhist principles of the property,
Tara Hall is an Alcohol and Smoke Free venue.

Q9: Is there on-site parking?There are 11 onsite parking,
and there are plenty of parking on Magill Road and nearby streets.
We advise allow time for offsite parking.
